## Sara Ouhaddou Portfolio (related to her mosaic alphabet style)

### Galerie Polaris (galeriepolaris.fr)
- **Al Kalima series** -- pigment on Japanese paper, calligraphic mark-making
- **Partition series** -- enameled ceramic inspired by Amazigh poetry
- **Stained glass mobiles** with geometric alphabet forms
- **Monumental silkscreen** (1200x160 cm) at Z33 Hasselt
- **Woven/Unwoven series** -- embroidery on rubber

### AWARE Women Artists
- "Je te rends ce qui m'appartient..." (Manifesta 13) -- archive, ceramic, soap
- "Sin Ithran, Ur Mqadan, Rousn" -- rotating stained glass, 130cm, closely related to geometric alphabet
- "Woven//Unwoven" -- embroidery on recycled rubber

### La Couleur du Verre -- "Lettres Ouvertes" Exhibition
- Five stained glass panels transcribing Berber women's songs in the invented alphabet
