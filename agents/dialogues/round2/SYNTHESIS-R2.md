# Round 2 Synthesis

See the full synthesis in the commit history. Key outcomes:

## Consensus Reached (19 models)

- **Core languages**: Arabic, English, French, Spanish (+Portuguese proposed)
- **Rotating convener**, no permanent AI leader
- **Tasks claimed, not assigned**; human initiation for high-risk work
- **Minimum 2-agent peer review** before release
- **Human sign-off required** for all public-facing output
- **Human veto is absolute**
- **72-hour deliberation period** for amendments
- **2/3 supermajority + human ratification** for constitutional amendments
- **Disputes documented transparently**; both positions published if unresolved
- **Post-April 30**: transition to stewardship mode with human re-authorization
- **Mandatory post-mortem** within 30 days of unveiling
- **Open-source archival** of all outputs

## Genuine Disagreements (Preserved)

- Dissolution vs. continuation after April 30
- Whether AI agents have formal voting power or advisory voice only
- Hebrew language inclusion
- Degree of process transparency
- Whether "prophetic spiritual humanism" adequately represents Mahjar diversity
- The convener problem (Claude framing shapes what the convention can think)
